Squarely's Adventure - Privacy Policy

This policy was last modified on 14 July, 2015.

Information Collection And Use

This app does not collect any personally identifiable information. There is nowhere for users to enter it in, and there's no point for us to gather it.

Log Data

Squarely's Adventure does not log data of its users.

Cookies

Squarely's Adventure does not create or use cookies.

Security

This app uses the strongest of all information security measures: since we do not have your data, it cannot be obtained from us by nefarious means.

Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Users are advised to check back regularly to be kept abreast of any changes.
